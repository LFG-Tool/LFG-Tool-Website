# LFG Tool v1.1.0 - Minor Update
## Karma Features

LFG Tool now lets you rate other players that you've played with.

---

## New Karma System

You can now interact with player reputation directly inside LFG Tool:

- `/karma` - Lets you view a player’s karma score. On the same message you'll be provided with buttons to upvote or downvote other people's score! You cannot vote on yourself. sorry.

---

## How it works

We recommend using this system honestly:
- Praise players you had good games with
- Down-vote players who were disruptive or unpleasant

We hope this feature encourages players to generally be nice and pleasant to others. If anyone gives you a hard time then go ahead and down-vote them! One thing that will not be allowed is targeted down-voting. You should only vote people based off your interactions not what interaction your friend had.

---

## What's coming next

We are planning to expand the karma system further, including:

- Follow ups system - The bot may DM you after a match asking how players behaved
- Join requests - Allows you to set your lobby to "request only" so you will be DMed who wants to join your lobby. Maybe someone with a very negative karma wants to join... you probably don't want that. 

---

## Abuse & feedback

If you encounter issues, have suggestions, or see abuse of the system (or any LFG Tool feature), please reach out via our discord https://discord.lfgtool.xyz

Any negative user interactions should be reported to the community you used LFG Tool within.
