# Why use LFG Tool?

LFG Tool is a Discord bot designed to streamline the Looking For Group experience for gaming communities, drastically reducing management overhead for staff.

In typical community servers, LFG channels are notoriously difficult to moderate and frequently attract scammers, hackers, and trolls. LFG Tool solves this problem through robust, automated moderation workflows and extensive back-end logging.

## Is LFG Tool right for my server?

Right now our system is based around **Steam games** which support `steam://joinlobby/` links. Lots of games actually support these links ([Learn more here](./usage/getting-a-join-link)) and eventually we'll fully support more games as requested. 


## Core Features

*Note: This feature list is continuously growing as development progresses.*

<details open>
<summary><b>Moderation Tools</b></summary>

- **Targeted Bans:** Ban problematic users specifically from creating LFGs or using Voice Channel (VC) services.
- **Comprehensive Logging:** Dedicated logging channels track VC transcripts, member renames, channel joins, creation, transfers, and ownership claims.
- **Activity Tracking:** Monitor individual user LFG history and track exactly who joined a specific group.
- **Pardoning System:** Built-in appeal framework to review and lift LFG or VC bans.
- **Channel Overflow:** Automatically deals with overflowing channels by creating a temp category, only denying once reaching 480 channels.
- **SteamID Blocking:** Block specific SteamIDs from posting or joining lobbies entirely.

</details>

<details open>
<summary><b>Looking For Group (LFG) Management</b></summary>

- **Game Branch Selection:** Support for specific game versions (e.g., Vanilla, Beta, Modded).
- **Customizable Posts:** Users can dynamically update their LFG title and description.
- **Flexible Join Methods:** Support for Steam Join Links, Lobby Codes, or direct DM requirements.
- **Access Control:** Banned users are entirely blocked from revealing join methods or links.
- **Post Refreshes:** Users can bump or resend their LFG post after a configured cooldown.
- **Post Deletion:** Allows users to easily delete their post if a link or method was entered incorrectly.
- **Specific game:** Branches can be configured to accept a steamID of a game to make sure none off-topic LFGing is happening.

</details>

<details open>
<summary><b>Dynamic Voice Channels (VCs)</b></summary>

- **Join-to-Create:** Automatically generates a temporary voice channel when a user joins a hub category.
- **Interactive Interface:** An intuitive UI panel for users to interact with and manage their channel settings.
- **VC Transcripts:** Generates text transcripts of voice interactions for security and review.
- **Privacy Controls:** Lock your channel or explicitly block specific users from entering.
- **Channel Customization:** Change VC names, set user capacity limits, or switch voice regions on the fly.
- **Management & Scaling:** Easily transfer channel ownership or claim an abandoned VC if the original owner leaves.
- **Staff Override:** Moderators retain visibility and access to all channels, ensuring no hidden spaces.
- **Automated Cleanup:** Empty channels are instantly deleted. If the bot restarts or goes offline, dead remnants are automatically purged.

</details>
